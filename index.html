Buildings
Buildings are the most basic, and 1st mechanic of the game. You buy classmates, teachers and professors to gain points. Later on they are boosted by other mechanics of the game.
Upgrades
Upgrades are the 2nd mechanic, you get enough points, and you get a boost based on what the upgrade has to offer.
Equations
Equations are the 3rd mechanic, you have the mathematical equation and graphical equation. Mathematical equation is just a normal maths equation like you see 1+x=2. If you are wondering why do you divide the x by 2, well if you pass the 1 on the right side you get 2-1 which is 1, and than x/2 = 1, than you need to multiply both sides by 2 to get x, which gets you to x=2, and that's your multiplier. Graphical equation is pretty simple, it's used for graphs of course, in school you would choose what ur x is while also given the k or n, but because this is a game of course you gotta buy the amount of x with Points.
Linear essence
Linear Essence is the 1st layer of this game. It is pretty simple, and even the name is linear. There is Linear upgrades that unlock new stuff, and boost your progress. Linear equation is a new equation and you get the result based on the equation logic. Buyables need to be purchased to increase the equations result. Later on you unlock Challenges.
Formula: 1.1^(y from 2nd equation/20 - 1) * 1.3^(log10(x from 1st equation))
Linear Challenges
Linear Challenges are the 3rd mechanic of Linear reset layer. They are pretty easy because the layer is linear and it's suppose to be easy. They are the last mechanic of this update.
