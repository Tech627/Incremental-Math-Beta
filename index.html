Guide
Formulas
Softcaps
Buildings
Buildings are the most basic, and 1st mechanic of the game. You buy classmates, teachers and professors to gain points. Later on they are boosted by other mechanics of the game.
Upgrades
Upgrades are the 2nd mechanic, you get enough points, and you get a boost based on what the upgrade has to offer.
Equations
Equations are the 3rd mechanic, you have the mathematical equation and graphical equation. Mathematical equation is just a normal maths equation like you see 1+x=2. If you are wondering why do you divide the x by 2, well if you pass the 1 on the right side you get 2-1 which is 1, and than x/2 = 1, than you need to multiply both sides by 2 to get x, which gets you to x=2, and that's your multiplier. Graphical equation is pretty simple, it's used for graphs of course, in school you would choose what ur x is while also given the k or n, but because this is a game of course you gotta buy the amount of x with Points.
Linear essence
Linear Essence is the 1st layer of this game. It is pretty simple, and even the name is linear. There is Linear upgrades that unlock new stuff, and boost your progress. Linear equation is a new equation and you get the result based on the equation logic. Buyables need to be purchased to increase the equations result. Later on you unlock Challenges.
Linear Challenges
Linear Challenges are the 3rd mechanic of Linear reset layer. They are pretty easy because the layer is linear and it's suppose to be easy.
Polygons
Polygons are the 4th mechanic of the game you buy a regular polygon from the 1st buyable. Proceed getting length after buying 1 regular polygon to increase your points. Continue like that and buy dimensions to increase in what dimension your polygon is! Polygon boosts get unlocked after you reach a certain amount of length. Dimensions after unlocking v-axis don't have much of a use but will when you unlocked the next mechanic comes so if you come to the v-axis there is no need to buy more dimensions.
Tangent
Tangent is the 5th mechanic of Linear reset layer. You got a lot going on so let me break it down for you. Left side you got everything about pi, middle about tangent and the circle arcs, and the right side is all about the equation of Tangent. The Circle challenge is the only thing you can do in the start. You enter it and your main button changes into The Circle button your upgrades get transformed into talmide upgrades instead. This is a new dimension, good luck with it. Tangent length is based on papirus/50,000. Also you can't get tangent length while in The Circle.
